Potato Bites

Page by: Jordan Patterson

Recipe from Rachel Ray

Ingredients:

  1. 4 red potatoes,
    sliced into 1/4-inch-thick rounds and patted dry
  2. 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  3. Salt
  4. pound bacon
  5. 1/3 cup sour cream
  6. 1/3 cup cream cheese
  7. 1 tablespoon finely chopped chives
